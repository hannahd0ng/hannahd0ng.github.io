“Designer Profile” Portrait: My self - portrait is a collage with a hand holding paintbrushes, symbolizing my art journey from childhood to graphic design. It mixes elements like newspaper textures and colors, reflecting diverse influences, similar to Hannah Höch’s work. Design Intent: Inspired by collage’s role in expression and cultural reflection, this portrait blends materials to show my designer identity. It challenges artistic boundaries, reflecting work - study balance and cultural experiences. Like Höch’s social commentary, it merges art and design to show modern identity, using collage’s power for personal narrative.
